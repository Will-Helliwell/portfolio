This is a custom-built recipe scraper that visits the recipe search index of BBC Good Food and iterates over each seach page to extract all the details (cook time, ingredients etc.) of every recipe on the site. These are then inserted into a database on MongoDB that is used by the Recipy App to populate its recipes.

All recipe rights belong to bbcoodfood.com. This project is solely for educational purposes.
